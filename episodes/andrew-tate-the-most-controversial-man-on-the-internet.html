Key Takeaways

  • Breathing controls your nervous system: longer exhales activate rest-and-digest; longer inhales activate alertness
  • Supplements like omega-3s, creatine, and vitamin D support brain function when diet is insufficient
  • Non-sleep deep rest (NSDR) can restore dopamine and mental energy in just 10-20 minutes
  • Your brain changes most when you fail and try again—neuroplasticity requires effort and errors
  • Dopamine is about motivation, not reward—it drives you toward goals and is depleted by too-easy pleasures
