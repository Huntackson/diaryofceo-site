Alex Hormozi: The Man That Makes Millionaires: How To Turn $1,000 Into $100 Million!

About Alex Hormozi: Alex Hormozi is an entrepreneur, investor, and author who has built and scaled multiple businesses generating over $200 million in annual revenue. He is the founder of Acquisition.com, which invests in and grows businesses, and the bestselling author of '$100M Offers' and '$100M Leads'. He is known for his no-nonsense approach to business growth.

In this episode, Alex Hormozi breaks down the exact principles and strategies he has used to build multiple businesses generating hundreds of millions of dollars. Alex starts with his core philosophy: the fastest way to make money is to make other people money.
